Have you ever thought to yourself: "damn if we weren't at an airport right now I would totally ask her out". Well, that's what this game's about. And since it's a game in 2025, might as well make it a soulslike. You are going on a singular date with a cute girl at the airport. Don't fuck it up and die. 

-- Click the enemies when close to hit -- attempting a hit uses stamina

Requires fullscreening the game, controls are just normal WASD. 

Made during Pirate Software Game Jam 2025. 

Everything created by me
